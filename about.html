Can you tell me about...


There are many wonderful people behind the game of OneShot.

FutureCatGames is the main team behind OneShot, OneShot: World Machine Edition, OneShot: The Pancake Edition, and Margin of the Strange. They were founded in 2017 a month after the release of the main version of OneShot. Originally they went by Little Cat Feet, but then later in 2020 they changed to the name they have now.

Outside of the main team, there are many other members who helped make the game, including...

Eliza Velasquez

One of the lead developers of OneShot. They worked on the code, writing, some of the music tracks for the game, and the designs of two characters, being George and Maize.

Find them here:

Michael Shirt

The other main developer of OneShot. Other than the code, they also work on debugging the game, writing, and produced some of the music tracks in game. They also did the animation of several in game things, including the text in one of the credit rooms, the water in the glen, and many other things.

Find them here:

NightMargin

The main artist, designer, and musician of the game. Other than that, they were also one of the writers of the game. They also make tons of promotional art for the game, designed many of the merch designs for the game, and make art consistently, even for other games.
